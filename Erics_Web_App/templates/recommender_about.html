About

This page tells you a bit about Meeple Finder Mediocre Board Game Recommender, where it came from and who it is intended for.

This project was inspired by my many trips to my local board game store, trying to figure out which board game I should buy or play next. With over 89,000 board games on boardgamegeek to search and choose from, I was having a hard time making a decision. In January 2017, I started a Data Science Immersive program at Galvanize in Seattle, WA, and for my final project, I made this site/model. This site is intended for people who are avid board gamers who want to branch out and try something new. I hope you enjoy the site. Check out the How it works tab to learn a bit more about what's going on behind the scenes.
